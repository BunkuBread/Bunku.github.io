Our Story

Bunku Bread is a home-based business that started with a love for baking and a dream to bring the taste of viral Korean bread to the UAE. ...

What began as a small kitchen experiment quickly grew into a local sensation, with friends and family raving about the soft, fluffy loaves and unique flavors.

Today, Bunku Bread is a beloved brand, sharing joy and deliciousness with every bite—all from the comfort of our home kitchen.
