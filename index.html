Our Story

Bunku Bread started with a love for baking and a dream to bring the taste of viral Korean bread to the UAE. ...

What began as a small kitchen experiment quickly grew into a local sensation, with friends and family raving about the soft, fluffy loaves and unique flavors.

Today, Bunku Bread is a beloved brand, sharing joy and deliciousness with every bite.

Founder

Meet Ahmed, the mastermind behind Bunku Bread. One day, he looked at a loaf of bread and thought, "What if this was fluffier?" The rest is history. Now he bakes, dreams, and sometimes even eats the bread.
